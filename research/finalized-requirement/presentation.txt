  Presentation Tips

  Timing Breakdown (12-15 minutes):

  - Slides 1-3 (Intro/Problem): 2 minutes
  - Slides 4-5 (UX/HCI): 2 minutes
  - Slides 6-7 (Tech/Algorithm): 2 minutes
  - Slides 8-9 (Features/Implementation): 3 minutes
  - Slide 10 (Demo): 3-4 minutes
  - Slides 11-13 (Challenges/Results/Future): 3 minutes
  - Q&A: 3-5 minutes

  Visual Design Recommendations:

  - Color scheme: Professional (blues/purples) with accent colors
  - Font: Sans-serif (Inter, Poppins, or Montserrat)
  - Images: High-resolution, annotated screenshots
  - Consistency: Same template throughout
  - Animations: Subtle transitions (avoid distractions)

  Delivery Best Practices:

  1. Start strong - Hook audience with the problem
  2. Tell a story - User journey narrative
  3. Show, don't just tell - Live demo is powerful
  4. Connect to HCI theory - Reference principles explicitly
  5. Be prepared - Have backup screenshots if demo fails
  6. Practice timing - Rehearse to stay within time limit
  7. Anticipate questions - Prepare for technical deep-dives
